Why Did We Build Edge Feed?

The market is flooded with over 20,000 news items every day from various sources. The vast majority of this is noise; very few stories are true catalysts for significant price volatility.

Traders consistently face several pain points:

  • Information Overload

    With 20,000+ daily headlines, manual tracking is impossible. You risk missing "golden setups" buried under mountains of noise.

  • Ineffective Filtering

    Standard filters fail to distinguish "rumors" from "facts." Wasting precious seconds verifying headlines costs you profit.

  • Fragmented Workflow

    Constantly switching tabs to monitor fragmented sources is slow. By the time you react, the price has likely already moved.

Core Features

  • AI-Powered Noise Reduction

    Our multi-layered NLP algorithms filter out 99% of noise in milliseconds, delivering only the top 1% of market-moving news directly to you.

  • Ultra-Low Latency Delivery

    Time is alpha. We push high-value alerts the instant they hit the wire, giving you a critical speed advantage over the market.

  • Smart Context Analysis

FAQs

  • How is Edge Feed Different from Traditional News Sites?

    Most traditional news sites focus on the news itself. They are weak on classification, core summarization, stock matching, and filtering. They can't push news to users with precision.

    When using a traditional news site or broker app, the user must actively pull all news for a stock or the market, then manually categorize and filter it.
    With thousands of listed companies, it's impossible to actively find that critical, first-hand information quickly.

    Edge Feed does the exact opposite. It ingests as much news as possible, matches it to a ticker, and if it's deemed valuable by the algorithm, passively pushes it to the user. For the trader, this means you are passively receiving high-value, pre-filtered news. This dramatically saves time and increases the efficiency of capturing high-value information.

  • How many alerts does Edge Feed send per day?

    The number varies daily, depending on the volume from upstream sources and the results of our algorithm.

    It could be anywhere from zero to several dozen.

  • What trading style is Edge Feed suited for?

    Edge Feed is designed for trading news-driven volatility. This means ultra short-term, "in-and-out" trades, often completed within minutes.

    It focuses primarily on small-cap stocks (under $1B market cap) and pushes alerts related to: Mergers/Acquisitions, Financing, Strategic Partnerships, and FDA-related news.

    Disclaimer: Edge Feed is a news aggregation and filtering tool. Nothing in its feed constitutes investment advice. Ultra short-term trading carries extremely high risk; please make decisions based on your own judgment.
