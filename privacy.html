Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to KairAlert. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website.

2. Cookie Policy

We use third-party analytics cookies.

KairAlert does not use first-party cookies to track your personal identity. However, we use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and performance. This third-party service may place a cookie on your device to collect anonymous usage data (such as page visits and dwell time) to help us improve the user experience.

3. Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (such as name, address, or phone number) unless you voluntarily submit it to us via email or Discord. We rely on third-party aggregators for news data.

4. Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party services including Google Analytics (for performance tracking), Discord (for community), and Twitter. These services operate under their own independent privacy policies.
